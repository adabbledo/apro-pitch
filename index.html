The revenue

Six layers. All recurring.

Labelling
Every product in every market needs compliant labels. Different jurisdictions, different rules, different languages. Per-label or per-market subscription. High volume, ongoing.
Translation
Regulatory documents, certificates of analysis, marketing materials — all need jurisdiction-specific translation. Per-document or bundled into market-entry packages.
Regulatory intelligence
Laws change. Markets shift. Operators pay to know what changed this month in Germany, Thailand, or Australia. 207 deterministic checks. Continuous subscription, high retention.
Network marketplace
Suppliers pay for verified listings and visibility. Manufacturers pay for introductions to compliant supply in new markets. The platform takes a cut of every connection it facilitates.
Strategic partner access
Major players — genetics companies, equipment suppliers, international brands — pay premium rates to reach the entire network. One integration, every market.
Exclusivity
Lock a region, a category, or a product type. The only certified lab in Thailand on the platform. The only cold chain provider in Germany. Scarcity compounds value.

More operators join, more valuable the network becomes. More suppliers list, more useful the platform is for manufacturers. The network compounds. The revenue compounds with it.
