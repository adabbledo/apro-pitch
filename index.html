The problem

Compliance never stops.
Neither do the costs.

Cannabis is the most heavily regulated consumer product on earth. Every operator — manufacturer, lab, cultivator, brand, distributor, pharmacy — lives under the same pressure. The scrutiny is constant. The rules are different everywhere. And the penalty for getting it wrong is losing your licence.

The Audit
An inspector calls. You have 48 hours to produce every batch record, supplier qualification, and deviation report from the last 12 months. If anything's missing, production stops.
The Rules
TGA in Australia. BfArM in Germany. Thai FDA. Each jurisdiction has different requirements for the same product. Rules change quarterly. Miss one and your export licence is at risk.
The Paper Trail
Batch records. COAs. Supplier audits. Deviation reports. CAPA logs. Stability data. Most operators manage this across seven different systems. Some still use paper.
The Cost
Consultants charge $5,000+ for a single compliance audit. Regulatory monitoring runs $2–5K per month. Label review: $1–3K per SKU per market. 61 hours a week on compliance admin. And you're still not ready when the auditor calls.
Every operator knows the audit is coming. Most spend weeks getting ready. What if you just… were?
